# FAQ

Frequently asked questions about Cypherpunk Code and how to use it.

---

## General

### What is Cypherpunk Code?

An open archive database of curated cypherpunk and cryptocurrency education resources. We index, score, and sequence freely available material — courses, papers, guides, documentation, and manifestos — with editorial judgment to cut trading noise.

### Is this a course platform?

No. We do not host courses or videos. We index and link to external resources, adding metadata, Cypherpunk Scores, and learning path sequences. Think of us as a wiki-style catalog with editorial curation.

### Is it free?

Yes. 100% free, permanently. No paywalls, no premium tiers, no accounts required.

### Do I need to create an account?

No. Browse the entire catalog, all learning paths, and all documentation without signing up.

### Is this financial advice?

No. This is an education index. Nothing here constitutes financial, investment, or legal advice.

## Studying

### Where do I start?

1. Read [How to Study (Bottom → Top)](../doc/how-to-study.md)
2. Follow the [Cypherpunk Foundations](https://cypherpunk-code.ca/paths#cypherpunk-foundations) path
3. Then move to [Bitcoin Sovereignty](https://cypherpunk-code.ca/paths#bitcoin-sovereignty)

### How long does it take to complete all paths?

At 30 minutes/day: approximately 6 months for all 6 paths. Most learners focus on 2–3 paths (3–4 months).

### Can I skip the philosophy and go straight to Bitcoin?

You can, but we do not recommend it. Understanding *why* privacy and sovereignty matter makes the technical material meaningful. The philosophy path is only 1–2 weeks.

### What is the Cypherpunk Score?

An editorial rating (1–10) for how relevant a resource is to privacy, sovereignty, and cryptography. See [Cypherpunk Score Explained](cypherpunk-score.md).

### How do I filter out trading content?

Set the CP Score filter to 7+ in the catalog. This removes most trading-focused content.

### What languages are supported?

The platform UI and resource metadata are available in English, French, Japanese, and Chinese (Simplified). Original resource content remains in its source language.

### How do I suggest a resource?

Contact [@CHxmrBrother](https://x.com/CHxmrBrother) on X with the URL and why it belongs in the archive.

### How do I report a broken link?

Contact [@CHxmrBrother](https://x.com/CHxmrBrother) on X. Include the resource name and what happens when you click the link.

### Is my browsing tracked?

No. We do not use user tracking, analytics cookies, or behavioral profiling.

### Can I export the catalog data?

Not yet — JSON/CSV export is on the [roadmap](../doc/roadmap.md) for Phase 3.

## Roadmap

### Will there be a mobile app?

The web platform is already mobile-responsive. A Solana Seeker dApp is planned for 2027+ as a secondary channel. See [Seeker dApp Strategy](../solana/seeker-dapp-roadmap.md).

### Will there be onchain certificates?

Yes — free verifiable certificates (Soulbound tokens on Solana) are planned for 2027. See [Feature Roadmap](../solana/feature-roadmap.md).

Yes. The catalog is maintained and expanded continuously. Community submissions will be formalized in Phase 2.

No. The platform will always be free for learners. See [Monetization](../solana/monetization.md) for how the project sustains itself.
