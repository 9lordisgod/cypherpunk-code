# Monetization (Without Charging Learners)

Cypherpunk Code is **100% free for all learners** — permanently. Revenue comes from channels that do not affect the learning experience.

---

## Core principle

> Learners never pay. The platform never paywalls. Education about freedom technology must itself be free.

---

## Revenue channels

| Channel | Description | Difficulty | Potential | Priority |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| **Sponsorships** | Sponsored modules or "Powered by" from wallets, exchanges, privacy projects | Medium | High | High |
| **Grants** | Solana ecosystem grants, privacy orgs, builder grants | Medium | High | High |
| **Affiliates** | Referrals to good wallets, tools, exchanges (clearly disclosed) | Easy | Medium | Medium |
| **Audience business** | Consulting, workshops, corporate training, paid newsletter | Medium | Very High | Medium |
| **Content** | YouTube + X long-form content driving traffic to platform | Medium | High | High |
| **Donations** | Direct BTC/XMR donations (already live) | Easy | Low–Medium | Ongoing |
| **NFT collection** | Community PFP collection (Phase 2) | Medium | Medium | Medium |
| **Future token** | Light points/token system (only after big community) | Hard | Very High | Low |

---

## Early focus (2026)

```
High-quality content + web platform
        ↓
Grow audience (X, Nostr, YouTube)
        ↓
Layer sponsorships + grants + affiliates
        ↓
Never charge learners
```

### Sponsorships

- Privacy-focused wallets (Wasabi, Samourai, Monero wallets)
- Node infrastructure providers (Umbrel, Start9)
- VPN and Tor-friendly services
- Hardware wallet manufacturers

**Rules:** Sponsored content is clearly labeled. Sponsors cannot influence CP Score or editorial decisions. No KYC-required sponsors.

### Grants

| Source | Fit |
| --- | --- |
| Solana Foundation | Interactive onchain learning features |
| Privacy-focused orgs | Cypherpunk education mission |
| Bitcoin developer grants | Open-source education tooling |
| Web3 education grants | Free curriculum development |

### Content strategy

- X threads explaining cypherpunk concepts → drive to platform
- YouTube walkthroughs of learning paths → drive to platform
- Nostr long-form posts → decentralized distribution
- All content links back to free platform resources

---

## What we will never do

| Anti-pattern | Why |
| --- | --- |
| Paywall courses | Violates core mission |
| Premium tiers | Creates two-class learners |
| Data selling | Violates privacy values |
| KYC-gated content | Excludes the people who need it most |
| Token-gated access | Speculation over education |
| Ad-heavy experience | Destroys learning focus |
| Trading signals | Not our mission |

---

## Sustainability model

```
Year 1 (2026):  Donations + grants + volunteer curation
Year 2 (2027):  + Sponsorships + content revenue + grants
Year 3 (2028):  + Audience business + affiliate + NFT/community
Long-term:      Self-sustaining via diversified non-learner revenue
```

The platform is designed to be maintainable by a small team. The catalog is data-driven (JSON files), the site is static/SSR, and hosting costs are minimal.

---

## Transparency commitment

All sponsorships, grants, and affiliate relationships will be:

- Disclosed on the About page
- Never influencing editorial CP Score ratings
- Reviewed quarterly for alignment with mission
- Removable if they compromise learner trust
